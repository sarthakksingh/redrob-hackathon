## Ranking implications

1. The pool is broad, so title and skill matching alone will be noisy.
2. The 5-9-year experience band should be a positive signal, not a hard cutoff.
3. Career-history descriptions should be the decisive evidence for production retrieval, ranking, evaluation, and product-shipping experience.
4. Behavioral signals should adjust a strong technical-fit score; they should not rescue a non-fit profile.
5. The next profiling pass should examine suspicious timeline and skill-duration combinations to detect honeypots.
